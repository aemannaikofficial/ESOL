ABOUT US

We aim to be a trusted partner in achieving English proficiency and opening the doors to global opportunities.

At ESOL Awards, we are committed to providing affordable English courses, live classes, and a groundbreaking LEB Secure English Language Test (SELT) Credit rated with entry onto the Scottish Credit and Qualifications Framework  database. Our tagline, “English for Global Opportunities,” reflects our deep-seated belief in the transformative power of English in today’s interconnected world.

Not confined by borders or backgrounds, our intended audience includes everyone who aspires to enhance their English skills and become a global citizen. Our responsibility is to ensure that opportunities and quality English learning are accessible to all, especially those with limited access to quality English learning resources.

We are a community of passionate learners and educators dedicated to advancing English language proficiency. Together, we will strive for excellence, embrace diversity, and foster connections from many individuals around the globe.

Please explore our courses, join our live classes, and begin your journey to English proficiency. We are here to support you every step of the way.
